# Better Data Partner Portal Guide

## Overview

The Partner Portal provides free, limited access for suppliers and partners invited by Better Data customers.

Partners can:
- View shared products and inventory
- Track inbound shipments
- Receive and confirm ASNs

Partners do not manage their own inventory unless they upgrade.

## How Partners Join

Partners are invited by a paying organization.

### Invitation Flow

1. Customer sends partnership invitation
2. Partner receives email with secure link
3. Partner accepts invitation
4. Organization is created with:
   - `planTier: FREE`
   - `partnershipTier: PARTNER_ONLY`
5. Partner is redirected to the Partner Portal

## Partner Portal Access (FREE)

### What You Can Do

✅ View shared products  
✅ View shared inventory (read-only)  
✅ Track shipments to your locations  
✅ Receive & confirm ASNs

### What You Cannot Do

❌ Create or edit products  
❌ Manage your own inventory  
❌ Create purchase orders  
❌ Use AI classification  
❌ Access SCM planning tools

## Partner Portal Navigation

- 🏠 **Dashboard** - Overview of partnerships and activity
- 🤝 **Partnerships** - View active partnerships
- 📦 **Shared Inventory** - Browse inventory shared by partners
- 🚚 **Shipments** - Track inbound shipments
- 📄 **ASNs** - Receive and confirm Advanced Shipping Notices
- ⚙️ **Settings** - Account and notification preferences

All data is automatically filtered based on partnership relationships.

## Upgrade Options

### PRO — $199/month

- Create and manage your own products
- Manage inventory at one location
- Create purchase orders
- Use AI-powered product classification
- Bulk imports (up to 1,000 SKUs)

### ENTERPRISE — $999/month

- Multi-location inventory
- Advanced AI forecasting
- API access
- Enterprise support

## AI Classification as an Upgrade Benefit

**FREE partners:**
- View categories on shared products (read-only)

**PRO & ENTERPRISE:**
- AI-powered category suggestions
- Bulk classification
- Conflict detection
- Cross-taxonomy mapping
